Number Guessing Game

Guess a number between 1 and 100:

Rules:

1. You earn 10 points for a correct guess in the first attempt, 5 points for a correct guess in 2 to 5 attempts, and 1 point for a correct guess in 6 to 9 attempts.

2. If you take more than 10 attempts to guess the correct number, 15 points will be deducted from your score.
